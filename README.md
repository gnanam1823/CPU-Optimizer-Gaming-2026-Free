## 📋 Understanding the settings

The software uses three main techniques to help your PC.

### Process Priority

Windows assigns tasks to your processor based on importance. Some background tasks take up space that your game needs. This tool tells Windows to treat your game as the main task. This gives your game priority over smaller chores your computer does for the system.

### Core Parking

Computers sometimes turn off parts of the processor to save electricity. This causes lag during sudden action in games. Core parking control ensures every part of your processor stays awake. This keeps your frames per second steady.

### Power Plan Optimization

Windows often places limits on your hardware to match your power settings. These limits prevent your processor from reaching top speeds. This tool enables the high-performance profile. This allows your hardware to use as much power as it needs for gaming.
